About Techno Tech Engineer

Techno Tech Engineer is a premier Industrial Fabrication Company based in Karad. We specialize in designing and manufacturing top-tier industrial components, machinery, and complete plant setups.

With a commitment to quality and precision, we deliver turnkey projects for various industries, including food processing and sugar (Jaggery) production, ensuring robust and efficient solutions tailored to your needs.

  • Expert Fabrication
  • Turnkey Project Management
  • High-Quality Materials
  • Timely Delivery

Why Choose TechnoTech?

We combine advanced engineering principles with precision craftsmanship to deliver unmatched industrial performance.

Precision CAD/FEA Design

Every system is simulated using advanced 3D CAD modeling and finite element stress analysis to guarantee absolute structural integrity.

Premium Material Selection

We enforce strict traceability, utilizing certified SS304/SS316 food-grade stainless steels and carbon steels that stand up to corrosive processing chemicals.

Turnkey Capabilities

From custom fabrication and heavy material lifting to mechanical assembly, piping systems, and thermal insulation, we execute the entire commissioning scope.

Robust R&D & Innovation

Our dedicated R&D laboratory engineers patent-pending thermal improvements, optimizing sugar extraction yields and fuel savings by up to 30%.
