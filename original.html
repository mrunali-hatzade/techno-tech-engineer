Welcome to Technotech Engineers

Technotech Engineers was created to provide a wide variety of industries with a complete source for a high volume complex fabrication of components and equipment.

We provide fabrication services to a wide variety of industries with complete source of manufacturing storage tank, jacked tank, heavy structure, chimney, elevator, conveyors, trolleys, vacuum pan, agitator tank, spinning machine, centrifuge, cutting machine, blender, kettle, complex fabrication and turnkey project in food processing industries.

Our engineering team has wealth of practical and theoretical experience and expertise. We have complete 3D modeling and 2D Detailing capabilities and are able to communicate with a wide range of software/drawing formats. Our administrative and management personnel have extensive experience and are completely dedicated to giving superior customer service and honest/open communication.

Our Products

We provide complete food processing plant & machinery solutions.  Technotech Engineers offer the most innovative, convenient and profitable single window solutions from design and manufacturing to erection and commissioning of complete raising processing plants, jaggery processing plants, waste management process house equipment, boiler non-pressure parts etc.

Turnkey Raising Plant

Technotech Engineers is a leading manufacturer of turnkey raising processing plant with concept to commissioning starting from 2ton/hr to 4ton/hr.

Jaggary Process Plant

Technotech Engineers manufacture and supply all jaggary process plant related equipment and all plant with concept to commissioning starting from 50ton/Day to 200ton/Day.
